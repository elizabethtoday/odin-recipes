Flag Cake

Description

You know you want a festive cake for the holiday weekend! But, do you wonder how to give the people what they want? An enticing festive addition to your July 4th table. This cake is just as fun to cut as it is to eat!

We will take a white cake and get creative by adding in some red and blue. This is guaranteed to make everyone smile! It is time to have a little bit of fun in the kitchen!

Ingredients

  • 3 packages white cake mix
  • 9 egg whites
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 3 3/4 cups whole milk
  • 2 tablespoons clear vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons red food coloring
  • 1 tablespoon blue food coloring
  • 1 cup white chocolate chips
  • 10 cups white frosting
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar or as needed
  • 14-inch round paper or cardboard stencil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Mix up the cake stuff, mix, eggs, oil etc
  3. Put half the batter into a separate bowl and add red food coloring; mix and fill 2 of the 8 inch pans
  4. Put half of remaining uncolored batter into next cake pan
  5. Use blue food coloring for the remaining batter and mix in the chips
  6. Bake 30 mins, cool and even tops and sides with a knife
  7. Cut Red and white cakes into 2 thin layers
  8. Use the stencils, cut the round pieces
  9. Assemble the cake, alternating the layers with frosting
  10. Frost the outside of the cake generously
  11. To serve, cut cake into wedges and enjoy the holiday!
