July Fourth Salad

Get ready for the best summer salad you could possibly imagine! This salad is made for everyone who says salad is boring! With all the things we love about fresh summer fruit berries mixed in and enticing us with every bite.

This salad has fresh red leaf lettuce for the base. And then, sprinkle in blueberries, strawberries crumbled feta cheese and sliced almonds. Drizzle the tasty dressing, and everyone will be coming back for salad seconds!
