July Fourth Salad

Get ready for the best summer salad you could possibly imagine! This salad is made for everyone who says salad is boring! With all the things we love about fresh summer fruit berries mixed in and enticing us with every bite.

This salad has fresh red leaf lettuce for the base. And then, sprinkle in blueberries, strawberries crumbled feta cheese and sliced almonds. Drizzle the tasty dressing, and everyone will be coming back for salad seconds!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 cup strawberry preserves
  • 1 tablespoon>white wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon dijon mustard
  • 1/4 teaspoon sea salt, or to taste
  • 1 head red leaf lettuce
  • 1 cup sliced strawberries
  • 1 cup blueberries
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 tablespoons sliced almonds

Steps

  1. Combine oil, preserves, vinegar, mustard and salt in bowl. Mix until combined.
  2. Wash, dry and spin lettuce. Use large leaves to line bowl, with red color to outside.
  3. Tear remaining leaves to fill bowl.
  4. Arrange berries, feta and almonds on top and drizzle with strawberry vinaigrette.
  5. Serve, eat and smile!
